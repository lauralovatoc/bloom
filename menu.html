Menu

All of our dishes are freshly prepared every day using high-quality ingredients.

Take a look at our delicious selection:

Parma Focaccia


Fluffy focaccia stacked with layers of Parma ham, creamy ricotta and fresh arugula. A savory, Italian-inspired sandwich perfect for lunch or a snack.

Cheescake


A rich and creamy classic New York-style cheesecake topped with a vibrant strawberry sauce. Smooth, indulgent, and perfect for dessert lovers.

Avocado Toast


Rustic sourdough topped with smashed avocado, a sunny-side-up egg, and a sprinkle of chili flakes. Fresh, wholesome, and packed with flavor.

Blackberry Pastry


Flaky puff pastry filled with a tangy berry cream and crowned with juicy blackberries. A sweet and tart pastry that’s perfect with coffee or tea.

Full Breakfast


A satisfying breakfast plate featuring a golden waffle, crispy bacon, and a sunny-side-up egg. A comforting and filling way to start the day.

Cinnamon Roll


Soft, gooey cinnamon roll swirled with buttery cinnamon sugar and finished with a smooth vanilla glaze. Warm, sweet, and utterly irresistible.

Eggs & Ham Bagel


Toasted bagel filled with fluffy eggs, savory ham, melted cheese, and greens. A satisfying, protein-packed option for breakfast or brunch.

Pan Au Chocolat


A buttery, flaky pastry filled with rich dark chocolate. A French classic perfect for a sweet breakfast or indulgent treat.

Tomato Toast


Grilled sourdough topped with whipped ricotta, heirloom tomato slices, olive oil, and herbs. A fresh and flavorful toast that's light yet satisfying.

French Toast


Thick slices of brioche soaked in a cinnamon-vanilla custard, pan-fried to golden perfection and topped with fresh berries and powdered sugar.

Omelet


Fluffy, golden omelet filled with cheese, herbs, and diced vegetables or bacon. A customizable classic, perfect for a hearty breakfast or brunch.

Croissant


A buttery, flaky French pastry with a crisp outer layer and soft, airy interior. Perfect on its own or paired with coffee.
